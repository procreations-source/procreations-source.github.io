Biography & More About Me

Anđela Novaković

My true love for photography originated in my early childhood. I love taking photos that evoke an emotional response and capture somebodies attention. Today I try to capture great moments through the camera lens and thus save them in time. In addition, I also photograph for commercial purposes.

I aim to photograph the landscape in a way that will help others appreciate and care for it. I hope to express the deep spirituality that I find in nature and to bring back images to people that might not otherwise have an opportunity to witness the beauty.

My inclusive and collaborative approach to work produces fun, genuine moments as they unfold. It keeps shoots spontaneous, light and full of energy, and to the best of my ability, will give a very natural, emotive final image.
